Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +
You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
How long does NFC scanning take? +
NFC scanning typically takes 2-5 seconds. Make sure the card is properly placed near the NFC reader and don't move it until the scan is complete. If the scan fails, try again after a few seconds.
What should I do if I forget my hospital admin password? +
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email. If you don't receive it within 5 minutes, contact our support team directly.
How do I track patient card delivery status? +
You can track card delivery status in the "Card Request Status" section of the hospital dashboard. All requested cards are listed with their current status (Pending, Sent, or Received).
Is there a fee for replacement cards? +
Yes, replacement cards for lost or damaged cards cost 600 DA. This fee is payable via Gold Card or debit card. The replacement card will be delivered within 7-10 business days.
